    Expedition Log

    Day 1 - The Launch

    Jul 29
    Commerce City, CO to Burley, ID (~8 hrs)

    Holiday Inn Express. Early morning departure through Wyoming and into Idaho.

    • Car games: License plate bingo through WY and ID
    • Look for the Continental Divide signs
    • Download offline Minecraft maps for the drive
    • Find a soccer field at a rest stop for quick juggling
    • Spot the Snake River crossing near Burley
    • Build challenge: Card house in the hotel room

    Day 2 - Northwest Bound

    Jul 30
    Burley, ID to Ellensburg, WA (~6.5 hrs)

    Holiday Inn Express. Crossing through Oregon into Washington state.

    • Watch for Boise skyline from the highway
    • Cross the Columbia River - one of the biggest in the West
    • Guitar practice in the car (chord shapes, strumming)
    • Ellensburg has a cool downtown to walk around
    • Look for wild horses in the eastern WA desert
    • Build challenge: Rubber band ball from gas station supplies

    Day 3 - Into the Forest

    Jul 31
    Ellensburg, WA to Lynden, WA (stop at Deception Falls)

    AirBnb. Stop at Deception Falls. Rehearsal dinner for Trevor's wedding at 5:50 PM.

    • Hike at Deception Falls - short trail, epic waterfall
    • Cross the Cascade Mountain range
    • Lynden is a Dutch-style town - look for windmills
    • Rehearsal dinner at 5:50 PM - suit up!
    • Build challenge: Mini dam at Deception Falls creek
    • Find cool rocks at the falls for your collection

    Day 4 - Wedding Day

    Aug 1
    Lynden, WA (all day)

    Pick blueberries in the morning, Trevor's wedding in Ferndale later.

    • Morning: Pick blueberries at Barbie's Berries
    • Eat as many blueberries as you pick (probably)
    • Trevor's wedding in Ferndale - celebrate!
    • Dance floor moves - show off your skills
    • Build challenge: Aztec pyramid from sugar cubes or crackers
    • See if there is a soccer field nearby for morning warmup

    Day 5 - Cross the Border

    Aug 2
    Lynden, WA to Vancouver, BC

    AirBnb. Cross into Canada! Baseball game tonight.

    • Cross the US/Canada border - have your passport ready
    • Baseball: Spokane Indians vs Vancouver Canadians
    • Nat Bailey Stadium is one of the coolest minor league parks
    • Try Canadian snacks (ketchup chips, Coffee Crisp)
    • Build challenge: Juggle a soccer ball - count your record
    • Spot differences between Canadian and American cities

    Days 6-7 - Vancouver Explorer

    Aug 3-4
    Vancouver, BC (Free Days)

    Two free days to explore one of the coolest cities in North America.

    • Stanley Park seawall walk/bike - 10km loop along the ocean
    • Granville Island - street performers, food market, cool shops
    • Science World - hands-on exhibits (building/tinkering heaven)
    • Gastown - steam clock, cobblestone streets, oldest part of city
    • Find a pickup soccer game at a beach park
    • Try poutine (fries, cheese curds, gravy) - a Canadian must
    • Capilano Suspension Bridge or Lynn Canyon (free option)
    • Build challenges: Tower from hotel items + paper airplanes

    Day 8 - Mountain Crossing

    Aug 5
    Vancouver, BC to Radium Hot Springs, BC (~8 hrs)

    Epic drive through the Canadian Rockies. Stay 2 nights.

    • Drive through the Fraser Canyon - dramatic mountain highway
    • Cross Rogers Pass in Glacier National Park (Canada)
    • Watch for mountain goats, bears, and eagles
    • Spot glaciers from the highway
    • Arrive at Radium Hot Springs - soak those legs!
    • Build challenge: Skip rocks at the lake - count your best

    Day 9 - Rest Day

    Aug 6
    Radium Hot Springs, BC (rest day)

    Chill day. Hot springs, exploring, good food.

    • Soak in the hot springs (mineral pools at 40C/104F)
    • Eat at Poutine Queen - loaded poutine!
    • Hike the Juniper Trail (easy, great views)
    • Watch for bighorn sheep in town (they walk the streets!)
    • Guitar practice by the pool
    • Build challenge: Mini catapult from popsicle sticks

    Day 10 - Montana Bound

    Aug 7
    Radium Hot Springs, BC to Billings, MT (~7 hrs)

    Holiday Inn Express East. Back into the USA, crossing Montana.

    • Cross back into the USA at the Montana border
    • Drive through Flathead Lake area (biggest lake in the West)
    • Montana is BIG - wide open spaces
    • Billings has a cool rimrock area (cliffs above town)
    • Build challenge: Balance challenge - stack weird things
    • Last night in a hotel - make it count!

    Day 11 - Homeward Bound

    Aug 8
    Billings, MT to Commerce City, CO (~9 hrs)

    The final leg. Through Wyoming and home to Colorado!

    • Last big driving day - you got this
    • Cross through Sheridan, WY and down I-25
    • Watch for pronghorn antelope in Wyoming
    • Colorado border - almost home!
    • Build challenge: Time capsule - write a letter to 13-year-old Tate
    • Reflect on the adventure - what was the best part?
